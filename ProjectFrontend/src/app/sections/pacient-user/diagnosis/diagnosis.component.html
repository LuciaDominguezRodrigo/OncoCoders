Diagnosis Results

The results obtained are a crucial step in the evaluation of the breast cancer diagnosis. Below are the main findings that should be analyzed by the specialist. Remember that these results should be discussed with your doctor for a better understanding.

  • Result 1: SA small anomaly was detected in the mammogram, which requires additional monitoring..
  • Results 2: Blood tests indicate an increase in biomarkers, which may suggest an elevated risk.
  • Results 3: Genetic testing suggests a hereditary predisposition to breast cancer.
  • Results 4: No other significant anomalies were found in the exams conducted
