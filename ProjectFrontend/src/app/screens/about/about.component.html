Code Pink: Technology and Innovation against Breast Cancer

Code Pink is a project driven by a multidisciplinary team from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC) and the Rey Juan Carlos University (URJC). Our goal is to revolutionize breast cancer research through the use of artificial intelligence and predictive models.

Our goal

We aim to improve diagnostic accuracy, facilitate early detection, and personalize treatments through the use of machine learning models. This will enable medical professionals to make faster and more informed decisions.

An Innovative Platform

We are developing an smart health web app that will combine the latest in technology and science to offer more precise, accessible, and personalized solutions. This tool will help doctors, researchers, and patients im prove diagnoses and treatments by providing advanced clinical and genetic data analysis tools.
